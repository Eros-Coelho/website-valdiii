The House of Drăculești

The house of Drăculești was founded by Vlad III's father, Vlad II of Wallachia, or Vlad Dracul. He originally descended from the noble House of Basarab, which had another branch called Dănești, to whom they were rivals.

The Order of the Dragon

According to some historians, the choice for the word "Dracul" comes from Vlad II's membership in the Order of the Dragon (founded in 1408). The Order's main purpose was to present a united front of the Christians in Europe against the Ottomans, and keep them out of the West.
